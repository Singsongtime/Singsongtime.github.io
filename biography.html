wyatt wakefield

biography

wyatt wakefield (b. 1994) was born in Morro Bay, California. Throughout his childhood lived in both the West- and East-Coast of the United States, having lived also in Philadelphia suburbs. He graduated from Morro Bay High School and has received education at Cuesta College in San Luis Obispo and at Temple University in Philadelphia. Currently, his aim is to compose music that relates to other arts, exploring the border-line with theater in particular.
