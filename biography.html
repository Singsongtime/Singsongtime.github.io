wyatt wakefield

biography


Wyatt wakefield (b. 1994) is an artist from the United States and is based in Graz, Austria, where he is studying the University of Music and Performing Arts Graz. His work is located within the performing arts, through concerts, music-theater, performance-installations, and happenings.
