Lucent Biography


Year: 2018

Performers: Jasmin Vorhauser, Christine Blasl

Location: Saint James's Church, Bergen, Norway
-----
-----


-----
-----
One of the longest english words with all unique letters is the word 'uncopyrightable'. I constructed a musical form relevant to this word and, through rehearsing closely with the musicians, relying on a large amount of open, verbal playing instructions (rather than notes), I attempted to return a sense of uncopyrightability to the musical form abstracted from the word uncopyrightable.
