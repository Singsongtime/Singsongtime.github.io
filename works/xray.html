XRAY

for organ, video, and grand pianos

--Year: 2019
--Performed by: Aleksey Vylegzhanin, organ // Tea Kulaš, piano // Milica Vujadinovic, piano // Wyatt Wakefield, video/projection
--Location: Institut für Kirchenmusik und Orgel, Kunstuniversität Graz



Videos tracing the text of a nighttime dream are projected/mapped onto the organ. The organ itself recounts the story through the sound of drones; morse code ('S.T.O.R.M'); a russian military march; a snowstorm of descending scales. We see landscape-textures; bodies ascending; a playboy-ballerina; and candid deer in infra-red.
