tin sundrops
Year: 2015
Performers: Anthony Ciesielka, Jessica Chambers, HM Mease, Wyatt Wakefield
Location: Rock Hall, Temple University, Philadelphia, USA
-----
-----
Two makeshift acoustic instruments, made from metal, duet. Stirring or shaking, their tones change, and a little water accidentally spills onto the floor. Someone lets a coin fall, strikes a bell, pours legumes from one cup to another. The fourth person types on a makeshift 'drum'. There are flowers on the table. This work can be read as an extension of certain paradoxical modes of practice made popular by John Cage in the 1950's, the composing of non-intentionality (by way of chance operations, graphic notation), the including of everyday objects into musical space, and 'silence'. If these practices operated to expand the definition of music, then simply repeating them some 60 years later appears a laughable affirmation that such strategies have crystalized as the same 'music proper' that they, in their own time, worked to expand.
