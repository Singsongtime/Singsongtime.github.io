nudists porn
Year: 2015
Performers: Anthony Ciesielka, Jessica Chambers, HM Mease, Wyatt Wakefield
Location: Rock Hall, Temple University, Philadelphia, USA
-----
-----
This work can be read as an extension of certain paradoxical modes of practice made popular by John Cage in the 1950's, the composing of non-intentionality (by way of chance operations, graphic notation), the including of everyday objects into musical space, and 'silence'. But in 2015? If these practices operated to expand the definition of music, then simply repeating them some 60 years later appears a laughable affirmation to the sense that such strategies have solidified as the same 'music proper' that they, in their own time, worked to expand. Is this a fate of conceptual art, that its residues become the same limits to which its coming into being depended on overcoming?
