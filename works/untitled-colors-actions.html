untitled 2018 (colors, actions)

for five performers, five laptops

Performers: Guy Ben-Tov, Anna Gluc, Eden Lonsdale, Wyatt Wakefield, Yulan Yu

Location: Kleinersaal, Kunstuniversit√§t Graz
Five bodies sitting at the same table respond to and are lit-up by bright colors on their individual computer screens determined by an aleatoric algorithm, like a game. The performers decided as a group as to which actions to use. An audience member saw the performers as being isolated from one another.
